Seattle has great coworking & maker spaces

This is a guide to those spaces. Use Space to get involved with and take advantage of the awesome spaces available in Seattle.

About the project

Space is a seattle.io project. The code is on GitHub and is MIT licensed. The spaces list is on Flatsheet and is CC by 4.0 licensed.

Contribute to Space

Interested in helping to maintain Space by writing descriptions, interviewing folks at coworking/maker spaces, taking photos, etc.? Email Seth Vincent at hi@seattle.io
